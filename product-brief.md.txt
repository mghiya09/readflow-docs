# Product Brief: ReadFlow

## Product Overview
ReadFlow is a web-based SaaS application that helps readers build consistent reading habits by setting goals, tracking progress, saving highlights, and reviewing reading insights.

## Target Users
* Casual readers seeking consistency.
* Knowledge workers (non-fiction).
* Students and lifelong learners.

## Key Features (v1 Scope)
1. **Reading Goal Creation:** Daily, weekly, or monthly targets.
2. **Progress Tracking:** Log pages or time by book.
3. **Highlight Management:** Save and categorize book excerpts.
4. **Summaries & Insights:** View reading patterns and AI-generated summaries.
5. **Data Export:** Export to CSV or Markdown.

## User Roles
* **Reader:** Standard user who tracks books and goals.
* **Admin:** Manages system settings and user accounts.

## Terminology
* **Reading Goal:** A target for pages or time.
* **Progress Entry:** A single update to a book's status.
* **Highlight:** A saved excerpt.

**Status:** Approved for documentation. Changes require scope review.
