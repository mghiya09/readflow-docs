# How to log and track reading progress
This article helps you log and track your reading progress.

## Prerequisites
You must have an active ReadFlow account.

## Log and track reading progress
To log and track your reading progress, do the following:

1. Log in to your ReadFlow account.
2. In your **Dashboard**, click **Log Progress**.
3. In the **Log Progress** modal, enter the following details:

    - **Book Title** - Enter the title of the book of which you want to track reading progress.
    - **Pages Read** - Enter the number of pages that you have read.
    - **Notes** - Enter any additional information. This is an optional step.

4. Click **Update Progress**. Your dashboard will immediately update to reflect your new page count and daily streak.
