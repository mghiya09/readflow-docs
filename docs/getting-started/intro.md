# Getting started with ReadFlow
Hey reader, welcome to **ReadFlow**. This guide helps you create your ReadFlow account and set your first Reading Goal to begin building your reading habit.

## Prerequisites
* A valid email address.
* A web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Safari recommended).

## Step 1: Create your ReadFlow account
First, you must create your ReadFlow account. To create an account:
1. Go to www.readflow.com
2. Click **Log In** in the top-right corner.
3. Click **Create an account** located at the bottom of the login window.
4. In the **Create an account** window, enter the following details:

   - **First Name** - Enter your first name
   - **Last Name** - Enter your last name
   - **Username** - Enter your desired username
   - **Email** - Enter your email ID
   - **Password** - Enter a unique password
5. Click **Create account**.
6. Open your verification email and click the link to activate your account.

![The ReadFlow Login and Sign-up screen showing the Create an account link](/assets/images/signup-page.png)

## Step 2: Set your first Reading Goal
Now that you have created your account, follow these steps to set your first Reading Goal.

A Reading Goal is the foundation of your consistency. It defines how much you want to read and how often. To set a Reading Goal, do the following:

1. Log in to your ReadFlow account.
2. In the home page, click **Create Reading Goal**.
3. In the **Create Reading Goal** window, enter the following details:
   - **Number of pages** - Enter your target (for example, 20)
   - **Frequency** - Select how often you want to hit this target (Daily, Weekly, etc.).
4. Click **Save Reading Goal**.

![The Create Reading Goal modal with fields for pages and frequency](/assets/images/create-goal-modal.png)

Congratulations, you have now successfully saved your Reading Goal.

Now, just enter the number of pages you read to track your reading progress.
